How to make a Sandwich

  1. go to the kitchen
  2. open cabinet
  3. grab ingredients = (peanut butter, jelly, bread)
  4. place ingredients on a flat surface
  5. grab utensils = (butter knife, small plate)
  6. place utensils next to ingredients on flat surface
  7. grab 2 slices of bread lay them on the plate
  8. open jelly and peanut butter
  9. use untensils to spread the ingredients
  10. put the 2 pieces of bread together
  11. and there you have a peanut butter jelly sandwich
  12. put ingredients and utensils away.
